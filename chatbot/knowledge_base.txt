Service Center Timings:
Our service center operates from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
The service center remains closed on Sundays and public holidays.
Customers are advised to visit at least 30 minutes before closing time for new service requests.

Warranty Policy:
Devices under warranty within 1 year of purchase get free repairs for manufacturing defects.
Warranty service requires a valid purchase bill, warranty card, or digital invoice.
Physical damage, water damage, accidental damage, cracked screen, burned components, and unauthorized repair are not covered under warranty.

Out-of-Warranty Policy:
Devices outside the warranty period are eligible for paid repair service.
Out-of-warranty customers must pay diagnostic charges, parts charges, and labor charges if applicable.
The final cost depends on the device model, complaint type, part availability, and repair complexity.

Diagnostic Charges:
Out-of-warranty diagnostics cost $20.
The diagnostic charge is waived if the customer proceeds with the repair.
If the customer does not proceed with the repair, the diagnostic charge is non-refundable.

Repair Timeline:
Standard repairs usually take 1 to 3 days.
Battery replacement usually takes 1 day.
Software reset or OS reinstall usually takes same day or 1 day.
Screen replacement usually takes 1 to 2 days.
Motherboard repair or part unavailability may take up to 7 days.
Repair time may increase if spare parts are not available.

Battery Issues:
If the battery drains fast, we recommend a battery health check.
Battery replacement costs around $50.
Battery replacement usually takes about 1 day.
Battery issues may happen due to old battery, background apps, faulty charging IC, poor charging habits, or damaged battery cells.

Screen Replacement:
Cracked screens are replaced with OEM parts.
Screen replacement cost ranges from $100 to $300 depending on the device model.
Physical screen damage is not covered under warranty.
Screen replacement usually takes 1 to 2 days depending on part availability.

Software Issues:
Software reset and OS reinstall are usually free under warranty.
Out-of-warranty software service costs around $30.
Software issues include app crashes, slow performance, update failure, boot loop, virus issues, and device stuck on logo.
Customers are advised to back up important data before software repair.

Charging Issues:
Charging problems may be caused by a damaged charging port, faulty adapter, damaged cable, battery issue, or charging IC failure.
Technicians will inspect the charging port, charger, cable, battery health, and charging IC.
If the charging port is damaged, part replacement charges may apply.

Network Issues:
Network issues include weak signal, WiFi not connecting, Bluetooth not pairing, SIM not detected, and internet disconnecting frequently.
Technicians will check SIM slot, network IC, WiFi module, Bluetooth module, and software network settings.

Speaker and Microphone Issues:
Speaker issues include low sound, no sound, crackling noise, distorted audio, and microphone not working.
Technicians will inspect the speaker, microphone, audio IC, and software audio settings.

Motherboard Issues:
Motherboard issues are treated as high-priority hardware issues.
Symptoms include device dead, automatic restart, no power, overheating, and power IC failure.
Motherboard repair or replacement may take up to 7 days depending on part availability.
Motherboard replacement cost depends on the model.

Repair Tracking:
Customers can track repair status using Customer ID and Phone Number on the service portal.
The repair status may be Open, In Progress, or Closed.
If the status is Open, the request has been registered.
If the status is In Progress, the technician is working on the device.
If the status is Closed, the repair has been completed.

Pickup and Delivery:
Customers must bring the service receipt or Customer ID while collecting the device.
Home pickup and delivery may be available for selected locations.
Delivery charges may apply depending on location.

Data Backup Policy:
Customers are responsible for backing up personal data before repair.
The service center is not responsible for data loss during software reset, OS reinstall, motherboard repair, or storage replacement.
Technicians may request permission before performing data-risk operations.

Refund Policy:
Diagnostic charges are non-refundable if the customer does not proceed with repair.
Parts once installed are not refundable.
Refunds are not provided after successful repair completion.

Escalation Policy:
If repair is delayed beyond the estimated timeline, customers can contact the service manager.
For urgent cases, customers should provide Customer ID, device model, complaint details, and phone number.
High-priority issues include motherboard issue, charging issue, and network issue.

Required Documents:
For warranty claims, customers must provide purchase bill, warranty card, or digital invoice.
For repair tracking, customers must provide Customer ID and registered phone number.

Customer Support:
Customers can contact the service center during working hours.
For support, customers should provide Customer ID, product brand, device model, complaint details, and phone number.
